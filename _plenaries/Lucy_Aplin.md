**Lucy Aplin** is an Associate Professor in the Research School of Biology at the Australian National University and a SERI Fellow in the Department of Evolutionary Biology & Environmental Studies at the University of Zurich. Her research focuses on interactions between cognition, sociality and ecology. She is especially interested in the emergent properties of social networks and culture.
